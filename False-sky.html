The Experiment

Have you ever questioned the sky above you? Have you ever seen something that shouldn't be there?

To participate, follow these steps:

  • Step outside at different times of day and **observe** the sky.
  • Look for distortions, glitches, or unnatural patterns.
  • At night, **speak the command:** "I see through the false sky."
  • Take note of any **immediate changes, shifts, or responses.**
  • Log your experience below.
